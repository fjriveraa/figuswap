⚽ FiguSwitch — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026
🌐 Leer en español

FiguSwitch ("the app", "we") is a tool for coordinating the trade of collectible stickers between users. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and who we share it with.

1. What information we collect

  • Email and password: to create and protect your account (or your Google account if you sign in with Google).
  • Your sticker collection: which stickers you have, need, or have duplicates of — the core content of your album in the app.
  • Your contact network: friend requests you send and receive within the app, used to calculate trade matches.
  • Your WhatsApp number (optional): only if you choose to enter it in your Profile. It is used solely so that contacts you've ACCEPTED in your network can message you directly when coordinating a trade. It is never used for advertising, never sold, and is not visible to anyone outside your accepted contacts.
  • Photos you upload to the Scanner: see section 2 below — handled differently from the rest of your data.

2. Photos and the AI Scanner

When you use the Scanner feature, the photo you take or upload is sent to a third-party AI service (Anthropic, maker of the Claude models) solely to identify which stickers appear in the image. FiguSwitch does not store that photo permanently — it is processed to detect the codes and then discarded. The photo is never shared with other users or used for any other purpose.

3. How we use your information

  • To show your album and keep it synced across your devices.
  • To calculate which stickers you can trade with your contacts.
  • To let you sign in securely.

We do not use your information for advertising. FiguSwitch does not display ads or sell your information to third parties.

4. Who we share information with

We use the following providers to operate the app — each receives only the information strictly necessary for its function:

  • Supabase: stores your account, album, and contact network in a secure database.
  • Vercel: hosts the web application.
  • Anthropic: processes Scanner photos solely to detect stickers (see section 2).

Other FiguSwitch users can only see your email and album if you accept a contact request from them. We never share your information with anyone outside these cases without your consent.

5. Security

Your album and contacts are protected by database-level security rules (Row Level Security), so only you — and contacts you accept — can see your information.

6. Your rights

You can request deletion of your account and all your information at any time from within the app (Profile → Delete my account), or by writing to soporte@figuswitch.com. More details on our account deletion page. We will process your request within a reasonable timeframe.

6.1 If you live in the European Union (GDPR)

In addition to the above, you have the right to:

  • Portability: request a copy of your information in a transferable format.
  • Objection: object to processing of your information in certain circumstances.
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate information about you.
  • Complaint: lodge a complaint with your country's data protection authority if you believe we have mishandled your information.

The legal basis for processing your information is the performance of the service you requested (keeping your account and album working) and, for the Scanner, your explicit consent when using that feature.

6.2 If you live in California (CCPA/CPRA)

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for advertising purposes, and never have. As a California resident, you also have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it's used (covered in sections 1 and 3 above).
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights.

7. Children

FiguSwitch is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under that age. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, contact us to have it removed.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. If we make significant changes, we will indicate so within the app.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, write to us at soporte@figuswitch.com.
